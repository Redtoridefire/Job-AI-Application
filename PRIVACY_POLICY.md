# Privacy Policy for Smart Job Application Autofill

**Last Updated:** November 23, 2024

## Overview

Smart Job Application Autofill ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data with external servers.**

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. We have no servers, no analytics, and no way to access your information.

## What Data is Stored Locally

The Extension stores the following data **only on your device**:

### Profile Information
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- LinkedIn URL
- Location (city, state)
- Work authorization status
- Resume text (if uploaded)

### Learned Responses
- Answers you've provided to application questions
- Field mappings for auto-fill

### Application History
- Job titles of positions you've applied to
- Company names
- Application dates and times
- Job posting URLs

## Where Data is Stored

All data is stored using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API, which means:
- Data stays on your computer
- Data is not synced across devices
- Data is not sent to any external servers
- We cannot access your data

## How Data is Used

Data is used exclusively to:
- Auto-fill job application forms
- Learn your preferences for future applications
- Track your application history
- Export your data for your own records

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT:**
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your data
- Send your data to external servers
- Track your browsing activity
- Use analytics or telemetry

## Permissions Explanation

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

- **storage**: To save your profile and preferences locally
- **activeTab**: To fill forms on the current tab when you click the extension
- **scripting**: To inject form-filling code into web pages
- **tabs**: To detect which job sites you're visiting
- **contextMenus**: To provide right-click menu options

## Data Control

You have full control over your data:
- **View**: All data is visible in the Extension's popup (Profile, Settings, History tabs)
- **Edit**: Modify your profile and settings at any time
- **Export**: Download your application history as CSV
- **Delete**: Clear learned data or history with one click
- **Uninstall**: Removing the Extension deletes all stored data

## Third-Party Websites

The Extension operates on third-party job application websites (like LinkedIn, Indeed, Workday, etc.). We do not control these websites' privacy policies. Please review their respective privacy policies for information about how they handle your data.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
